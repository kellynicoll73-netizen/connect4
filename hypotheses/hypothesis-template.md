# Crafting Good Hypotheses
## Hypothesis Structure
> "We believe [user segment] experiences [problem] when [context] because [underlying cause]"
---
### Specific
Clear about who, what, when, and why. Avoid vague generalizations.
### Falsifiable
Can be proven wrong through evidence. Frame to be disproven, not just confirmed.
### Consequential
If wrong, it significantly impacts your solution approach or strategy.
